Our Cause

To eradicate illiteracy and to promote lifetime learning opportunities among the youth and other learners to enable them make informed decisions, become selfreliant, and improve their livelihood to competitively compete in the modern world so as to impact positively on their communities sustainable

To offer Kenya National Curriculum. 8.4.4. Education to all.

To offer Quality Secondary Education and Life Skills Training to the youth

Establish a Resource Center to promote Open Schooling.

To provide skills to learners through formal and informal means.

To help meet social, spiritual, mental and physical needs of the Students
